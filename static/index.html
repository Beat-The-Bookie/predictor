Predictions 2024/2025

Welcome to Predictions 2024/2025!

Hi, thanks a lot for joining the FootPred beta. This is the first test release to see how the system works, and to get some feedback on how it feels. Thank you for having a look, and creating an account. For now, all you can do is change your predictions, and save or cancel those changes. Please do test these functions, and have a look at the leaderboard to see who else has joined! After a while, I will introduce a deadline, and then will leave it a couple of weeks and announce a winner, so it may actually be worth doing the predictions properly. So, once again, thank you for joining this test, and please send any feedback to footpredinfo@gmail.com.

Below is a list of planned features to be added in the future. If you think of anything else you would like to see, please let me know!

  • A league system, with custom settings such as how many teams to predict in each league.
  • Automatic odds, to offer a little help as you enter your predictions.
  • The ability to view your scores on a week-by-week basis, after the deadline.
  • View others' predictions, after the deadline. (To laugh at, maybe?)

Stay tuned! There will be future updates, and more feedback requests!

All things going well, there should be a release in July 2025
