Welcome to Beat the Bookie

Think you have ball knowledge? Prove it! Predict the final standings for Europe's top leagues — the Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Ligue 1, and the Championship.

Drag and drop the teams in each league to lock in your predictions. At the end of the season, you'll score points based on how close your predictions were to reality.

You will be competing against all other players for a £10 Amazon Voucher! After the deadline, the leaderboard will display everyone's scores throughout the season.

Also create custom mini-leagues with friends! You choose how many teams to score per league, with score updates throughout the season!

And the twist? You’re not just playing each other. You’re up against The Bookie — our in-house prediction account based on real betting odds. Can you Beat the Bookie?

If you have any questions, concerns or feedback, please contact beatthebookieinfo@gmail.com

The Story

The concept of predicting league standings is something that has been happening on a small-scale for a number of years. We began in the 2016-17 season by noting down our predictions in our secondary school planners, and spending time at the end of the season manually counting the scores. After two years, in 2018-19, someone created a spreadsheet to store the predictions, as it was much more reliable than a piece of paper. From 2021-22, I took over the spreadsheet and automated it as much as possible. Then, following the start of the 2024-25 season, with some knowledge of Supabase and web development, this public website was the clear next step. Skip forward 9 months, hundreds of hours, 4 different names, lots of different designs, and Beat the Bookie was born!
